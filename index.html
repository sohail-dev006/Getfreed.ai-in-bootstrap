Instant clinical notes tailored to you

Notes in your style, 10x faster

Freed learns your style and format, with every edit. Get customized clinical notes in moments, not hours.

Works in every setting

Capture notes accurately for any specialty visit up to 2 hours, be it virtual or in office, even if it’s noisy.

Easy to use

Copy and paste into your “favorite” EHR. Support your patient with easy to follow patient instructions.

Off the charts simplicity

1. Capture

Select “Capture visit”’ when your visit begins. Freed can listen for up to two hours, whether it's a virtual or office visit..

2. Edit

Select “End visit” and view your completed SOAP note in about a minute. Edit to help Freed learn your style. .

3. Sign off

Send simple patient instructions, and copy completed notes into any EHR.
